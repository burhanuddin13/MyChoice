What's My Type?
Card #1

Honestly, many factors go into a person's physical appearance. I wouldn't say that I am unaffected by their physical appearance. Everyone wants a good-looking partner. But I do not have a definitive list of things I consider beautiful. Beauty is subjective; hence, what others may think beautiful/hot may not be for me and vice versa.
